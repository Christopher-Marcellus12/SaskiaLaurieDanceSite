About Saskia Laurié

Saskia Laurié, known as Kilau, was born and raised in Brooklyn, where she began her dance training at Victory Music and Dance and Dancewave. She continued her studies as a dance major at LaGuardia Arts High School, graduating in 2022. In 2019, she participated in the MOVE|NYC| Young Professionals Program and trained at Ballet Hispánico.

In 2022, Saskia performed in the UK with the MOVE|NYC| and Scottish Ballet Youth Exchange, showcasing a piece choreographed by Maleek Washington. She also apprenticed with Camille A. Brown & Dancers in early 2023 and 2024.

Currently, Saskia is a junior at Hofstra University, pursuing a double major in Dance and Speech-Language-Hearing Sciences. At Hofstra, she has performed works by Jesse Obremski, Doug Varone, Telly Fowler, and Kizuna Dance. Additionally, she serves as the Head Coach of Creativz, a BIPOC dance team, and is the President of Hofstra's NAACP Chapter.
